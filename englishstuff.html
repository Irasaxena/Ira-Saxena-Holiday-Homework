English: Book Report (The Hungry Tide by Amitav Ghosh)

Home English French Science SSC
"The Hungry Tide" by Amitav Ghosh is a compelling novel set in the Sundarbans, exploring the lives of Piyali Roy, a marine biologist studying Irrawaddy dolphins, Kanai Dutt, a translator uncovering his uncle's writings, talking about his journey before he died; and Fokir, a local fisherman. The narrative intertwines themes of environmental conservation, identity, and human resilience, highlighting the delicate balance between human life and wildlife. With advanced vocabulary, Amitav Ghosh clearly conveys the story. His unique writing style makes this book educational and interesting to read, as it richly describes the Sundarbans' landscape and delves into the complex interplay between nature and human existence.
